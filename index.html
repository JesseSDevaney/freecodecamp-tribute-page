Carl Sagan's Life

  • 1934: Carl Sagan is born
  • 1955: Bachelor of Science Degree in Physics
  • 1960: Ph.D. Degree in Astronomy and Astrophysics
  • 1961: Becomes assistant professor at Harvard
  • 1963: Starts work at the Smithsonian Observatory
  • 1970: Becomes full professor at Cornell
  • 1972: Assembles 1st physical message sent into space
  • 1980: TV show Cosmos: A Personal Voyage airs
  • 1981: Humanist of the Year Award
  • 1985: Science fiction novel "Contact" published
  • 1995: Pale Blue Dot: A Vision of the Human Future in Space
  • 1996: Dies of pneumonia

Additional info about Carl Sagan can be found on the website dedicated in his honor, carlsagan.com.
